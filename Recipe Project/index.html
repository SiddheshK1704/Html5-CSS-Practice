Gulab Jamun:

Ingredients:

  • All-Purpose flour
  • Ghee
  • Milk
  • Cardamom powder
  • Rosewater
  • Sugar
  • Water

Recipe:

  1. Mix all-purpose flour, cardamom powder, and ghee, then add milk to form a smooth dough.
  2. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water, then add rosewater and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Roll the dough into smooth balls and set aside.
  4. Fry the balls in hot ghee until golden brown, then soak in the sugar syrup for 15-20 minutes.

Kaju Katli:

Ingredients:

  • Cashews
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Saffron strands
  • Pistachios

Recipe:

  1. Grind cashews into a fine powder using a grinder.
  2. repare sugar syrup by heating sugar and water together until it reaches a one-thread consistency.
  3. Mix cashew powder into the syrup, cook on low flame, and add ghee and cardamom powder.
  4. Set the mixture in a greased tray, flatten it, garnish with saffron and pistachios, then let it cool before cutting into pieces.
